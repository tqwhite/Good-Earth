It's time to start thinking summer! And Dog Days Westonka 2012 on August 18th 2012. 



Celebrate Greater Mound!  Dog Days Westonka will take place on Saturday, August 18, 2012.  “Dog Days Westonka” proceeds will to benefit pets in many ways. Assistance through local WeCAN (wecan-help.org), the Westonka Food Shelf. And another a pet waste station installation on a local trail.

Thanks to all the exhibitors, demos, volunteers, sponsors, and everyone that came to visit Dog Days Westonka was a huge success. A great turnout for a great cause! We look forward to seeing you again this year.

We are working on some new contests, a parade and informative demonstrations for this year. Check back soon for more information.
